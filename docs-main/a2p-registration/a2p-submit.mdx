---
title: "Submit A2P Carrier Registration"
description: "How to submit registration with MessageDesk."
---

You'll be prompted to complete A2P Registration when you add your first phone number to MessageDesk. 

You can start this process by clicking `Add Phone Number` from the Inbox side navigation or inside of Settings -\> Phone Management.

For help completing registration please email us at [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) or text us at 775.313.9465.
