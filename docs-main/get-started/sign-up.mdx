# Sign Up for MessageDesk

Create your account, verify your identity, and name your workspace—done.

---

## Requirements

- **Valid email address**
- **Personal mobile phone number** (for verification; not used as a workspace number)
  - International phone numbers are supported for account creation and login

<Warning>
  **Note:** Landlines, Google Voice/Skype/virtual numbers **won’t** work for verification.
</Warning>

---

## 1. Create Your Account

If you’re new (and haven’t [been invited to an existing workspace](/get-started/join-workspace)), go to the sign-up page and enter your **email** and **password**, or choose **Sign in with Google**.

![Create Account Pn](/docs-main/images/create-account.png)

**Password must include:**

- At least **1 uppercase** letter
- At least **1 number**
- At least **1 symbol**

---

## Verify via Mobile (SMS code)

You’ll receive a **6-digit code** by SMS to your personal mobile number. Enter the code to continue.

![Confirm Sign Up Pn](/docs-main/images/confirm-sign-up.png)

<Warning>
  Didn’t get the code? Email [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) or text [**(775) 313-9465**](sms:7753139465).
</Warning>

---

## Verify Your Email (email code)

Next, we’ll send a **second 6-digit code** to your **email**. Enter the code to finish verification.

![Verify Sign In Email Pn](/docs-main/images/verify-sign-in-email.png)

<Warning>
  Didn’t get the email? Check spam/junk, then contact [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) or text [**(775) 313-9465**](sms:7753139465).
</Warning>

---

## Name Your Workspace

Give your new workspace/inbox a **name** to complete the setup. You can change this later in **Settings**.

![Inbox Name Pn](/docs-main/images/inbox-name.png)

---

## What Happens Next?

- You’ll land in your **new workspace**.
- Connect a **phone line** (host a landline/VoIP, purchase a local/toll-free, or connect Twilio).
- **Invite teammates** and set **roles & permissions**.

---

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Is my personal mobile number used for texting customers?**\
No. It’s only used for **account security**. Your workspace will use **separate business numbers** you connect or purchase.

**Can I change my workspace name later?**\
Yes. Update it anytime in **Settings → Workspace Profile**.

**I was invited to a workspace—do I still use this page?**\
Follow the **invitation link** you received to join the existing workspace: see [**Join a workspace**](/get-started/join-workspace).
