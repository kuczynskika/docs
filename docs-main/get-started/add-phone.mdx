## Add a New Phone Number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings" iconType="regular" stepNumber={1} titleSize="h3">
    - Click your profile image In the top right of MessageDesk
    - Click "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Phone Management" stepNumber={2} titleSize="h3">
    - From the left hand navigation, select "Phone Management" under Workspace Settings
      - **\_note: \_**if you don't see `Phone Management` you may not have access. Speak with your account administrator for more details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click `Add Phone Number` " stepNumber={3} titleSize="h3">
    - Click `Add Phone Number` button in top right corner of Phone Management screen
    - Select your preferred phone number choice
  </Step>
</Steps>

![Settings Phone Management Pn](/images/settings-phone-management.png)

After clicking `Add Phone Number` you'll see the following dialog. You can select any option.

![Add Phone Dialog Pn](/images/add-phone-dialog.png)

## Texting with Multiple Phone Numbers

MessageDesk lets you add unlimited phone numbers to your inbox. You'll be able to manage every phone number from a single unified inbox.

You'll also have the ability to organize conversations by phone number. This way you can see everything at a glance or drill down to a specific set of conversations

![Inbox Sidebar Phones Pn](/images/inbox-sidebar-phones.png)

For Teams, you can assign or revoke phone number access for each individual teammate. Making it easy to customize which teammates have access to which conversations.

## Phone Number FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How many phone numbers can I add?" icon="phone" iconType="solid">
    You can add unlimited phone numbers to MessageDesk. Each phone number will need to be connected to an existing SMS Registration before you can start texting.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I text right away?" icon="message" iconType="solid">
    As soon as the phone number is connected to MessageDesk, you'll begin receiving all text messages.

    To reply to text messages or send your first message, you'll need to register the phone number and complete A2P Registration. This process is required.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I connect my existing landline?" icon="question" iconType="solid">
    Yes, you can complete phone number hosting to connect your existing landline phone.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Does every Teammate see every conversation?" icon="question" iconType="regular">
    No, you can limit teammate access by assigning them access to specific phone numbers or limiting their access to only specific conversations within a phone number.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
