## How to Connect a New Landline or VoIP Phone Number

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings" iconType="regular" stepNumber={1} titleSize="h3">
    - Click your profile image in the top right of MessageDesk
    - Click "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click Phone Management" stepNumber={2} titleSize="h3">
    - From the left-hand navigation, select "Phone Management" under Workspace Settings,
      - **I**f you don't see, `Phone Management` you may not have access. Speak with your account administrator for more details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Click `Add Phone Number` " stepNumber={3} titleSize="h3">
    - Click `Add Phone Number` button in the top right corner of the Phone Management screen
    - Select **Host existing phone number **
  </Step>
</Steps>



After clicking, `Add Phone Number` you'll see the following dialog. You can select any option.



## Processing time

After you submit your Letter of Authorization (LOA), our team processes the hosting request on the backend. Processing time varies by provider:

- **Typical timeline:** 5 minutes to 72 hours

<Note>
  **Adding additional numbers later?** You'll need to complete a new LOA for each number you want to add. The same processing timeline applies.
