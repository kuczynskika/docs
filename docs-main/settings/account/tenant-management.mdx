# Tenant Management

A **tenant** is the parent company account that owns one or more MessageDesk **workspaces**. Tenant admins can update tenant-level company details and spin up new workspaces for different teams, brands, or business units—all from a single account.

<Note>
  **Tenant vs. Workspace:** A tenant is your company. A workspace is an inbox under that company. Most users only need one workspace, but larger organizations can run multiple workspaces under the same tenant.
</Note>

---

## Who Can Manage a Tenant

Only users with the **tenant admin** role can:

- Edit the tenant company profile.
- Create new workspaces under the tenant.

Workspace-level admins (without tenant admin) can manage their own workspace but cannot edit tenant details or add new workspaces.

---

## Update Tenant Company Details

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right) and open the **workspace switcher**.
2. Select **Manage tenant** (visible to tenant admins only).
3. Click **Edit** on the **Tenant profile**.
4. Update your **company name**, **address**, **website**, or **logo**.
5. Click **Save**.

Changes apply at the tenant level and are reflected across all workspaces under the tenant.

---

## Create a New Workspace Under a Tenant

Tenant admins can create additional workspaces directly from the workspace selection screen—no need to sign up for a separate account.

1. From the **workspace switcher** or **workspace selection screen**, click **Create workspace**.
2. Enter a **workspace name** (e.g., "Sales", "Support", "West Coast Team").
3. Click **Create**.

When the workspace is created:

- You're automatically added as the workspace **admin**.
- A new membership is created linking your user to the workspace.
- The workspace appears under your tenant in the workspace selection screen.

<Tip>
  After creating a workspace, follow the standard onboarding steps to [connect phone numbers](/get-started/phone-numbers/overview), [import contacts](/contacts/create-import), and [invite teammates](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management).
</Tip>

---

## Workspace Selection Screen

When you sign in, the workspace selection screen groups your memberships by **tenant**. If you belong to multiple tenants or multiple workspaces under the same tenant, you'll see them organized accordingly:

- **Tenant header** — company name and logo.
- **Workspaces list** — every workspace under that tenant where you're a member.
- **Create workspace** — visible only if you're a tenant admin for that tenant.

Click any workspace to enter it, or use the workspace switcher inside the app to move between workspaces without signing out.

## Frequently Asked Questions

**Can a single user belong to multiple tenants?**\
Yes. If you've been invited to workspaces across different companies, each tenant appears as its own group on the workspace selection screen.

**Who becomes the tenant admin?**\
The user who originally created the MessageDesk account is the default tenant admin. Additional tenant admins can be assigned by an existing tenant admin.

**Can I delete a workspace under a tenant?**\
Workspaces can't be deleted from the UI. [Contact support](/contact-us) to request workspace deletion. Tenant deletion follows the same process.

**Does creating a new workspace require a separate subscription?**\
Billing for additional workspaces under the same tenant depends on your plan. See [Billing & Subscriptions](/settings/workspace-settings/billing-subscriptions) or contact support for details.
