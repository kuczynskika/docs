# Personal Profile Settings Overview

Set up your profile so teammates recognize you, tags populate correctly, and security features (like 2FA) work as expected.

<Note>
  **Mobile users:** On mobile, you can update your user profile information. Other settings features are desktop-only.
</Note>

---

## TL;DR

- Go to **Account → Settings → Profile** (My Settings).
- Add your **photo**, **name**, **email**, and **mobile**.
- Click **Save changes**.
- You can also **reset your password** here.

---

## Update Your Profile Details

### 1. Open your profile

**Account → Settings → Profile** (My Settings) → **Profile** tab.

### 2. Add or edit details

- **Profile image** – shows next to your messages and comments
- **User name** – how teammates see you in the app
- **First name / Last name** – used in templates/tags (e.g., `{{ UserFirstName }}`)
- **Email** – for login and account notifications
- **Cell phone** – recommended for **two-factor authentication (2FA)**
- **Country** – improves locale defaults

### 3. Save

Click **Save changes**.

<Note>
  Keeping **First name** and **Workspace name** up to date ensures your **personal signature** and **message templates** render correctly.
</Note>

---

## Manage Your Password

You can **reset your password** from the **Profile** tab.\
Choose a strong, unique password and store it securely.

<Info>
  For added security, MessageDesk enforces 2FA after adding your **Cell phone**.
</Info>
