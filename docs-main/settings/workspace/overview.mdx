# Workspace Settings Overview

Workspace Settings store **organization-level** info that applies to every user in your MessageDesk inbox—name, branding, address, and website.

---

## Open Workspace Settings

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right).
2. Select **Settings**.
3. In the left nav, open **Workspace Settings**.

<Warning>
  **Desktop only:** Workspace Settings are currently available on desktop. On mobile, you can access your **personal** preferences (appearance, notifications) only.
</Warning>

---

## What You Can View & Edit

**General**

- **Workspace image/icon** (used across the app)
- **Workspace name** (shown to teammates and used in tags like `{{ WorkspaceName }}`)
- **Mailing address**
- **Website URL**

**Identifiers (read-only)**

- **Created date** (displayed for reference)

<Note>
  **Who can edit?** Admins—and Managers if permitted by your [**Roles & Permissions**](/settings/workspace/team-management)—can update Workspace Settings.
</Note>

---

## Update General Information

1. Go to **Workspace Settings → General**.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Update your **image/icon**, **name**, **address**, or **website**.
4. Click **Save**.

![Workspace Settings General Pn](/docs-main/images/workspace-settings-general.png)

<Note>
  Changes to name/branding take effect immediately for all users.
</Note>

---

## Delete a Workspace

Workspaces cannot be deleted by users from the UI.

To request workspace deletion, please [**contact support**](/contact-us). We’ll verify ownership and process the request according to our data-retention and security policies.
