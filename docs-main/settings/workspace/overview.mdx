# Learn About Workspace Settings

Your workspace settings include your organization name, workspace ID, and other account-level information that applies to all users in your workspace.

## How to Access Workspace Details

To access your workspace details:

1. Click **Account** (your profile icon in the top right corner)
2. Select **Settings**
3. See **Workspace Settings**

<Warning>
  **Warning**: Access to workspace details and settings is currently limited to **desktop only**. Mobile users may only access [appearance settings](/settings/personal/overview) for their personal inbox on mobile.
</Warning>

## General Workspace Information

Your workspace name appears throughout MessageDesk and helps identify your organization.

To update your workspace name and other info:

1. Go to **Workspace Details \> General**
2. Click to **Edit** your workspace image/icon, name, mailing address, and website URL
3. Enter the new details

![Workspace Settings General Pn](/docs-main/images/workspace-settings-general.png)

<Note>
  **Note:** The date your workspace was created is displayed for reference.
</Note>

## Delete Workspace

MessageDesk does not allow users to delete workspaces.

To delete your MessageDesk workspace, please [Contact support](/contact-us) for assistance.
