# Workspace Profile

The **Workspace Profile** page is where you manage your workspace's identity: name, address, website, and branding. These fields also feed **A2P/carrier registration**, so the info you enter here should match what you submitted to the carriers.

---

## Open Workspace Profile

Go to **Account → Settings → Workspace Settings → Workspace Profile**.

---

## Fields you can edit

- **Workspace name.** Shown to teammates throughout MessageDesk and used in personalization tags like `{{ WorkspaceName }}`.
- **Address.** Your business address. Used as the registered address for compliance and carrier registration.
- **Website.** Your company website URL. Referenced during carrier registration.
- **Logo / branding.** Upload a workspace logo for in-app branding.

After updating, click **Save changes**.

---

## Who can edit Workspace Profile

Your role controls access:

| Role         | Workspace Profile access |
| ------------ | ------------------------ |
| **Admin**    | Owner (full edit)        |
| **Manager**  | Viewer                   |
| **Operator** | Viewer                   |

See [Roles & Permissions](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management) for the full default permissions table and how to customize roles.

---

## Keep this consistent with carrier registration

<Warning>
  Your Workspace Profile feeds **A2P 10DLC** and **toll-free** carrier registration. Changes to your **legal business name**, **address**, or **website** after you've registered may require a resubmission or update with the carriers to keep your messaging compliant.
</Warning>

If you need to update registration details, see [Carrier Registration → Overview](/carrier-registration/overview) and the [Compliance Center](/settings/workspace-settings/compliance-center).
