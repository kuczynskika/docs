# Workspace Settings Overview

Workspace Settings store **organization-level** info that applies to every user in your MessageDesk inbox: name, branding, address, website, and analytics.

<Note>
  **Workspace Settings vs. Tenant Management.** Workspace Settings (this section) configure a **single workspace**: labels, templates, team, billing, and other per-workspace details. **Tenant Management** is an **organization-level** concept that lives **outside** Settings, accessed from the **workspace switcher / workspace selection screen**, and is only available to tenant admins. Use it to update tenant company details or create additional workspaces under your company. See [Tenant Management](/get-started/tenant-management).
</Note>

---

## Open Workspace Settings

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right).
2. Select **Settings**.
3. In the left nav, open **Workspace Settings**.

<Warning>
  **Desktop recommended.** Most Workspace Settings are available on desktop. Some (like Analytics) are also available on mobile.
</Warning>

---

## What you can view & edit

**General**

- **Workspace image/icon** (used across the app).
- **Workspace name** (shown to teammates and used in tags like `{{ WorkspaceName }}`).
- **Mailing address**.
- **Website URL**.

**Custom Fields**

- **[Custom Fields](/settings/workspace-settings/custom-fields).** Create and manage custom fields to store extra data on contacts (customer IDs, account types, appointment dates).

**Analytics**

- **[Workspace Analytics](/settings/workspace-settings/analytics).** View messaging metrics, delivery rates, and activity breakdowns by channel and user.

**Identifiers (read-only)**

- **Created date** (shown for reference).

<Note>
  **Who can edit?** Admins, and Managers (if permitted by your [Roles & Permissions](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management)), can update Workspace Settings.
</Note>

---

## Update general workspace information

1. Go to **Account → Settings → Workspace Settings → Workspace Profile**.
2. Click **Edit**.
3. Update your **image/icon**, **name**, **address**, or **website**.
4. Click **Save**.

<Note>
  Changes to name/branding take effect immediately for every user.
</Note>

## Cancel a subscription vs. delete a workspace

**Subscriptions and workspaces are separate.** Cancelling a subscription doesn't delete a workspace, and deleting a workspace isn't tied to your billing cycle.

| Action                    | Where          | What happens                                                                                                                                                        |
| ------------------------- | -------------- | ------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Cancel subscription**   | In-app         | Severs the subscription ↔ workspace link at the end of the billing period. The workspace enters a **locked state** and stays that way until you resubscribe. Data is retained. |
| **Delete workspace data** | Support only   | Permanently removes workspace data. You email a request from a workspace admin address and verify the card on file. Not reversible.                                 |
| **Remove a teammate**     | In-app         | Revokes a single user's access. The workspace, its data, and its subscription stay intact. See [Team Management](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management).     |

<Note>
  **We retain workspace data indefinitely.** Cancelling a subscription leaves your data in place. If you resubscribe later, the workspace unlocks with everything intact.
</Note>

To cancel a subscription, see [Cancel subscription](/settings/workspace-settings/billing-subscriptions#cancel-subscription).

## Delete a workspace

Workspace deletion is a **support-only, permanent** action. There is no in-app button to delete a workspace.

### When to request deletion

- You want workspace data **permanently removed** from MessageDesk. If you only want to stop paying, [cancel the subscription](/settings/workspace-settings/billing-subscriptions#cancel-subscription) instead. Your data stays intact, and you can reactivate the workspace by resubscribing.

### How to request deletion

To authorize a workspace deletion, email [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) with the following:

1. **Send from an email tied to an admin user** on the workspace.
2. **Workspace name** and **tenant** (if you have more than one).
3. **Last 4 digits of the card on file** for verification.
4. (Optional) Request a **final data export** before deletion. See [Data Center](/settings/workspace-settings/data-center#message-history) for what can be exported.

<Warning>
  Workspace deletion is **permanent and can't be undone**. MessageDesk removes conversations, contacts, phone-line configuration, and settings. If you want to keep numbers you host or purchased through MessageDesk, [port them away](/settings/workspace-settings/phone-management#port-your-number-away-from-messagedesk) first.
</Warning>

<Tip>
  If your tenant has multiple workspaces and you only want to remove one, tell support which workspace to delete. Other workspaces under the same tenant aren't affected. See [Tenant management](/get-started/tenant-management).
</Tip>
