# Workspace Settings Overview

Workspace Settings store **organization-level** info that applies to every user in your MessageDesk inbox—name, branding, address, website, and analytics.

<Note>
  **Workspace Settings vs. Tenant Management:** Workspace Settings (this section) configure a **single workspace**—labels, templates, team, billing, and other per-workspace details. **Tenant Management** is an **organization-level** concept that lives **outside** Settings, accessed from the **workspace switcher / workspace selection screen**, and is only available to tenant admins. Use it to update tenant company details or create additional workspaces under your company. See [Tenant Management](/get-started/tenant-management).
</Note>

---

## Open Workspace Settings

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right).
2. Select **Settings**.
3. In the left nav, open **Workspace Settings**.

<Warning>
  **Desktop recommended:** Most Workspace Settings are available on desktop. Some settings (like Analytics) are also accessible on mobile.
</Warning>

---

## What You Can View & Edit

**General**

- **Workspace image/icon** (used across the app)
- **Workspace name** (shown to teammates and used in tags like `{{ WorkspaceName }}`)
- **Mailing address**
- **Website URL**

**Custom Fields**

- **[Custom Fields](/settings/workspace-settings/custom-fields)** — Create and manage custom fields to store additional data on contacts (e.g., customer IDs, account types, appointment dates).

**Analytics**

- **[Workspace Analytics](/settings/workspace-settings/analytics)** — View messaging metrics, delivery rates, and activity breakdowns by channel and user.

**Identifiers (read-only)**

- **Created date** (displayed for reference)

<Note>
  **Who can edit?** Admins and Managers (if permitted by your [**Roles & Permissions**](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management)**)** can update Workspace Settings.
</Note>

---

## Update General Workspace Information

1. Go to **Account** **→ Settings → Workspace Settings → Workspace Profile**.
2. Click to **Edit**.
3. Update your **image/icon**, **name**, **address**, or **website**.
4. Click **Save**.

<Note>
  Changes to name/branding take effect immediately for all users.
</Note>

## Delete a Workspace

Users cannot delete workspaces from the UI.

To request workspace deletion, please **get in touch with support**. We’ll verify ownership and process the request according to our data-retention and security policies.
