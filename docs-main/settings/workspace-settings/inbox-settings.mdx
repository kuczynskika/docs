# Inbox Settings

The inbox-related workspace settings—**Labels**, **Message Templates**, and **Relays**—each now have their own dedicated settings page. Use the links below to jump to the one you want.

<Note>
  Workspace settings act as **defaults**. **User preferences always override** workspace defaults and are **not** overwritten by later workspace changes.
</Note>

---

## Labels

Manage your workspace's shared **label library**—create, edit, and delete labels (name, color, icon) that the whole team can apply to conversations.

→ [Labels](/settings/workspace-settings/labels)

For how labels are **applied** inside conversations and used in filters, see [Inbox → Labels](/inbox/labels).

---

## Message Templates

Manage your workspace's shared **message templates**—pre-approved, reusable messages with optional descriptions, tags, and media that any teammate can pull into a conversation.

→ [Message Templates](/settings/workspace-settings/message-templates)

For how templates and **personalization tags** work when composing a message, see [Messaging → Templates & personalization tags](/messaging/templates-tags).

---

## Relays

Manage workspace **Relays** (automations) from the Relays module or from Workspace Settings—update status, edit triggers/conditions/actions, or delete a Relay.

→ [Relays overview](/relays/overview)

To configure the webhook endpoint and signing secret used by the **Send Webhook** relay action, see [Developer Tools](/settings/workspace-settings/developer-tools).

---

## Permissions

Admins (and Managers, if allowed by your [**Roles & Permissions**](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management)) can update these workspace settings. Each linked page lists the specific access required for that resource.
