# Billing & Subscription Management

View your plan, update payment methods, download invoices, and change your MessageDesk subscription, all from **Account → Billing**.

---

## TL;DR

- Go to **Account → Billing** to see **plan**, **next charge**, **seats**, **billing cycle**, and **invoice history**.
- You can **add/update a payment method**, **download invoices**, and **cancel** from the same page.
- To **change plans** (upgrade/downgrade), [contact support](/resources/support). Upgrades prorate immediately. Downgrades apply on your next billing cycle.

---

## Open your billing settings

1. Click your **profile icon** (top-right).
2. Choose **Account → Billing**.

![Billing Pn](/images/billing.png)

## Manage your subscription

### View current plan & billing details

The Billing page shows:

- **Plan name** and **price**.
- **User seats (quantity)**.
- **Billing cycle** (monthly or annual).
- **Next billing date**.
- **Billing contact:** name, email, address, phone.
- **Invoice history**.

<Note>
  **Seat minimums.** The **Team** plan needs at least **3 user seats**. The **Pro** plan has no seat minimum. See [Pricing](/resources/pricing) for plan details.
</Note>

![Billing Portal Pn](/images/billing-portal.png)

### Change plans (upgrade or downgrade)

To change plans, [contact support](/resources/support).

- **Upgrades.** Take effect immediately and **prorate**.
- **Downgrades.** Take effect on your **next billing date**.

## Payment methods

### Supported options

- **Card** (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover).
- **Bank (ACH)**.

### Add or update a payment method

1. Go to **Account → Billing**.
2. Click **+ Add payment method**.
3. Enter your details.
4. Click **Add**.

<Tip>
  Set a new method as **default** during or after adding it.
</Tip>

### Remove a payment method

You must have **at least one** active payment method on file.

- Add a new method first, then remove the old one.

## Billing information & history

### Update billing contact info

On **Account → Billing**, edit **name**, **email**, **address**, or **phone** in the **Billing information** section, then **Save**.

### View & download invoices

1. Go to **Account → Billing**.
2. Scroll to **Invoice history**.
3. Click any invoice to **view** or **download** (PDF).

## Tax-exempt status

If your organization is exempt from sales tax (nonprofits, government agencies, educational institutions, tribal entities, First Nations, foreign diplomats, and similar), MessageDesk can apply your exemption to future invoices.

### How to submit your exemption

1. Email a signed copy of your exemption document to [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com).
2. Send from an email tied to a **workspace admin** so we can match the request to your account.
3. We'll apply the exemption on our end. **Future invoices** will render without sales tax.

<Note>
  We don't automatically refund sales tax already charged on **prior invoices**. If you need a retroactive adjustment, mention it in your email and we'll review it case by case.
</Note>

### What document to send

A **W-9 is not a sales-tax exemption certificate.** Send the state- or country-specific document below.

**United States**

| Situation | Document to send |
| --- | --- |
| 501(c)(3) nonprofit | Signed state **Sales Tax Certificate of Exemption** (or **Blanket Exemption Certificate**), or your **IRS 501(c)(3) determination letter** if your state accepts it |
| Reseller | State-issued **resale certificate** |
| Government / educational / tribal | State-issued exemption certificate for your entity type |

<Tip>
  Most US states publish an exemption certificate form. For example, Ohio's **STEC B, Sales and Use Tax Blanket Exemption Certificate**. Use the certificate issued by the state where your organization is registered.
</Tip>

**Canada**

| Situation | Document to send |
| --- | --- |
| Indigenous / First Nations exemption (GST/HST/PST) | Valid **Certificate of Indian Status** card |
| Non-resident claiming exemption under a tax treaty (e.g. US–Canada) | **Certificate of Residency** (US residents: **IRS Form 6166**), articles of incorporation, and IRS determination letter |
| Foreign diplomat | **Exemption / Identity Card** issued by Global Affairs Canada |

## Cancel subscription

Cancelling a subscription **stops billing**. It doesn't delete your workspace or your data.

1. Go to **Account → Billing**.
2. Click **Cancel subscription**.
3. Confirm **Cancel subscription**.

Your workspace stays active until the **end of the current billing period**. After that, MessageDesk severs the subscription ↔ workspace link and the workspace enters a **locked state**. Your data is retained for **90 days**. If you resubscribe within that window, the workspace unlocks with everything intact.

<Warning>
  **Cancelled workspaces are automatically deleted after 90 days.** If you don't resubscribe within 90 days of cancellation, the workspace and its data are permanently deleted. If you want to delete the workspace **sooner** than 90 days, see [Delete a workspace](/settings/workspace-settings/overview#delete-a-workspace).
</Warning>

<Note>
  **Subscriptions and workspaces are separate entities.** Cancelling a subscription doesn't immediately delete the workspace. It enters a locked state and is deleted 90 days later if you don't resubscribe.
</Note>

## FAQ

**When will I be charged?**\
On the **same day each month** (monthly plans) or **once per year** (annual plans). Your **next billing date** shows in Billing.

**What happens if my payment fails?**\
We'll **retry** the charge and send an **email**. Update your **payment method** to avoid service interruption.

**Can I get a refund?**\
Refunds are handled **case-by-case**. Contact support to review your situation.

**Can I switch plans later?**\
Yes. [Contact support](/resources/support) to upgrade or downgrade. Upgrades **prorate immediately**. Downgrades apply on your **next cycle**.

**Do you support sales tax exemption? Can I submit a W-9?**\
Yes, we support sales tax exemption. Email a signed **state- or country-specific exemption certificate** to [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) from a workspace-admin email, and we'll apply it to future invoices. For US 501(c)(3) nonprofits, that's a Sales Tax Certificate of Exemption or your IRS determination letter. For Canadian First Nations exemptions, it's a Certificate of Indian Status. A **W-9 alone isn't a sales-tax exemption**. We need the state- or country-issued exemption document. See [Tax-exempt status](#tax-exempt-status) for the full list.
