## Overview

The Data Center is your central hub for managing data imports and workflows in MessageDesk. Use this workspace setting to import contacts and other data into your account.

## Contact Import

Import contacts into your MessageDesk workspace to quickly build your contact list.

### How to Import Contacts

<Steps>
  <Step title="Navigate to Settings">
    - Click your profile image in the top right of MessageDesk
    - Click "Settings" from the dropdown menu
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select Data Center">
    - From the left hand navigation, select "Data Center" under Workspace Settings
    - **Note:** If you don't see `Data Center`, you may not have access. Speak with your account administrator for more details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start Contact Import">
    - Click the "Import Contacts" button
    - Follow the prompts to upload your contact file
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Import Workflows

The Data Center will support various import workflows to help you manage your data efficiently. Additional import options will be added over time.

## FAQs

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What file formats are supported for contact imports?" icon="question" iconType="regular">
    Contact import supports standard CSV and Excel file formats. Make sure your file includes columns for phone numbers and contact names.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Who can access the Data Center?" icon="question" iconType="regular">
    Access to the Data Center is controlled by workspace permissions. Admins and Managers have access by default. Custom roles can be configured to grant or restrict access.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
