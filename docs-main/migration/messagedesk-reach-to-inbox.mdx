# **Why migrate from MessageDesk Reach to Inbox by MessageDesk?**

Migrating to Inbox by MessageDesk gives your team a messaging tool built for collaboration, control, and clarity.

- **Superior media support**\
  You don't have to tell users to "just email it." V2 lets you send and receive PDFs, DOCs, JPEGs, PNGs, GIFs, WEBPs, HEICs, and more. Files larger than ~500 KB automatically convert to links, and all attachments are managed in one place.
- **Group texting and 1:1 conversations**\
  Start a 1:1 conversation or include up to 7 recipients in a group text, without duplicating effort or context.
- **User access controls and customization**\
  You're not locked into a one-size-fits-all inbox. Inbox by MessageDesk lets you set up custom user roles and permissions, comment internally, @mention teammates, and tailor Inbox views. Dark mode, custom theming, and saved filters round out a workspace that adapts to how your team works.
- **Better organization and routing**\
  In Inbox by MessageDesk, you can label conversations freely, filter, route, or assign messages as a unified team.

## **Migration time at a glance**

- **Total time:** ~5-10 minutes.
- **Who's involved:** An account owner/admin and any teammates who will use Inbox.
- **What moves:** Your existing phone number (after you complete the setup steps).

### **What you'll need to migrate your account**

- The email address you want to use for your Inbox login.
- A list of teammates to invite (names and emails).

## How to migrate your MessageDesk Reach account

### 1. Log in to your MessageDesk Reach account

Visit [app.messagedesk.com](http://app.messagedesk.com) and log in to your existing account.

<Frame>
  ![Messagedesk Reach Login](/images/messagedesk-reach-login.png)
</Frame>

### 2. Visit Settings → Migrate to Inbox by MessageDesk

You may need to scroll in your settings list to view this option.

<Frame>
  ![Migration Settings Migrate](/images/migration-settings-migrate.png)
</Frame>

### 3. Click Connect with Inbox by MessageDesk

This begins the account linking process.

<Frame>
  ![Migration Connect Account](/images/migration-connect-account.png)
</Frame>

### 4. **Create your Inbox account and connect it to your Reach account**

Follow the link to visit **[https://inbox.messagedesk.com/#/create-account](https://inbox.messagedesk.com/#/create-account).** Here, you'll create your Inbox by MessageDesk account (or sign in to your existing Inbox account if one already exists).

<Warning>
  **Important:** Be sure to create your new Inbox account using the **same admin email** already associated with your MessageDesk Reach account.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![Migration Connect Account Email](/images/migration-connect-account-email.png)
</Frame>

### 5. Migrate your data

Click 'Start Data Migration' to begin moving the following data types to your new Inbox by MessageDesk account:

- **Contacts** (including addresses and email information)
- **Messages** (including media attachments)
- **Message templates**
- **Custom fields**
- **Groups** (which become [labels ](/settings/workspace-settings/labels)in Inbox by MessageDesk) 
- **Opt-out lists** (contacts who have opted out of communication)

<Note>
  **Note:** Every data type maintains its original structure and metadata
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Migration Data](/images/migration-data.png)
</Frame>

### 6. Begin Data Migration

Click 'Start Migration' to begin your data migration.

<Note>
  **Note:** Data migration runs in the background and does not affect your current MessageDesk Reach account. 
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Migration Data Start](/images/migration-data-start.png)
</Frame>

### 7. Monitor your data migration

You will receive various email notifications from [no-reply@messagedesk.com](mailto:no-reply@messagedesk.com) for each migrated data type. These will notify you when your migration begins and concludes. 

<Note>
  Each migration email will also include any notes on failures which you can pass to our dev team for investigation.
</Note>

<Frame>
  ![Migration Email Notifications](/images/migration-email-notifications.png)
</Frame>

### 8. Migrate your phone number

Next, you'll need to migrate your phone number from MessageDesk Reach to Inbox by MessageDesk to start sending and receiving messages.

<Warning>
  #### **Important Information**

  - Phone migration is instant.
  - The your MessageDesk Reach app will no longer send or receive texts. 
  - All text messages will begin running through Inbox by MessageDesk.
  - You can always move your number back from Inbox by MessageDesk if you choose but you must contact [**support@messagedesk.com**](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) to do so.
</Warning>

<Frame>
  ![Migration Phone Number](/images/migration-phone-number.png)
</Frame>

<Warning>
  **Note:** Only account admins can migrate phone numers from MessageDesk Reach accounts to Inbox by MessageDesk accounts.

  <Frame>
    ![Migration Admin Only Number Migration](/images/migration-admin-only-number-migration.png)
  </Frame>
</Warning>

### 9. Confirm your phone number migration is complete

You can verify your phone number migration's success from your MessageDesk Reach account and by logging in to your Inbox by MessageDesk account.

#### Migration complete in MessageDesk Reach

<Frame>
  ![Migration Phone Complete](/images/migration-phone-complete.png)
</Frame>

#### Migration complete in Inbox by MessageDesk

<Frame>
  ![Migration Inbox Migration Complete](/images/migration-inbox-migration-complete.png)
</Frame>

## Frequently Asked Migration Questions

### How many times can I run a data migration?

Once every 24 hours

### Do I need to be an admin to run a data migration

No, users with manager-level permissions can migrate data, but only admins can migrate phone numbers. Operators are unable to migrate data or numbers.

### Do I lose my MessageDesk Reach account?

No, your MessageDesk Reach account isn't deleted, and data can be moved to Inbox by MessageDesk multiple times. Your original account stays accessible and functional.

### Can I test Inbox by MessageDesk before moving my number over?

Yes. If you want to use Inbox by MessageDesk before moving your number, you can, and it's highly recommended. Moving your number to Inbox by MessageDesk is instant, but moving the number back to Reach requires messaging support.

### What data is migrated during the migration process?

The migration transfers your contacts (including addresses and email information), messages (including media attachments), message templates, custom fields, groups (which become labels), and opt-out lists. Every record keeps its original structure and metadata.

### How long does the migration process take?

It depends on how much data you have. Data migration runs in the background, and you'll get a notification when it's done. Phone number migration is instant once initiated.

### Can I use both my MessageDesk Reach and Inbox by MessageDesk accounts at the same time?

Yes. Use both platforms at the same time until you migrate your phone number. Once your phone number is migrated, every text message goes through Inbox by MessageDesk, but your Reach account stays accessible for viewing historical data.

### What happens if I need to move my phone number back to MessageDesk Reach?

You can always move your phone number back from Inbox by MessageDesk if you choose. Moving the number back requires contacting support.

### Will there be any downtime during migration?

No. There's no downtime during the data migration process. Your MessageDesk Reach account stays fully functional. Phone number migration happens instantly.

### What if something goes wrong during migration?

Contact [support@messagedesk.com](mailto:support@messagedesk.com) immediately. Your data stays safe in your MessageDesk Reach account, and you can retry the migration. Data migration can run multiple times if needed.

### Do I need to do anything after the migration is complete?

Once data migration completes, verify that your contacts, messages, and templates transferred correctly in Inbox by MessageDesk. When you're satisfied, migrate your phone number whenever you're ready.

### Will my team members and permissions be migrated?

No. Team members and user permissions are managed separately in Inbox by MessageDesk. Invite your team to your new Inbox by MessageDesk workspace and configure their permissions there.
