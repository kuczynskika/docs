# **Why migrate from MessageDesk Reach to Inbox by MessageDesk?**

Migrating to Inbox by MessageDesk gives your team a messaging tool built for collaboration, control, and clarity.

- **Superior media support**\
  You don't have to tell users to "just email it." V2 lets you send and receive PDFs, DOCs, JPEGs, PNGs, GIFs, WEBPs, HEICs, and more. Files larger than ~500 KB automatically convert to links, and all attachments are managed in one place.
- **Group texting and 1:1 conversations**\
  Start a 1:1 conversation or include up to 7 recipients in a group text, without duplicating effort or context.
- **User access controls and customization**\
  You're not locked into a one-size-fits-all inbox. Inbox by MessageDesk lets you set up custom user roles and permissions, comment internally, @mention teammates, and tailor Inbox views. Dark mode, custom theming, and saved filters round out a workspace that adapts to how your team works.
- **Better organization and routing**\
  In Inbox by MessageDesk, you can label conversations freely, filter, route, or assign messages as a unified team.

## **Migration time at a glance**

- **Total time:** ~15–30 minutes to prepare. Number migration is scheduled during business hours (see Step 5).
- **Who's involved:** An account owner/admin and any teammates who will use Inbox.
- **What moves:** Your existing phone number (after you complete the setup steps). Contacts can be exported/imported.

### **What you'll need to migrate your account**

- The email address you want to use for your Inbox login.
- A list of teammates to invite (names and emails).
- A preferred **day and time (Mon–Fri, 9 am–5 pm)** for number migration.

## How to migrate your MessageDesk Reach account

### **1. Create your Inbox account**

1. Visit [**https://inbox.messagedesk.com/#/create-account**](https://inbox.messagedesk.com/#/create-account)
2. Create your account (or sign in if one has been created for you).

<Warning>
  Use your email already associated with your MessageDesk Reach account
</Warning>

### **2. Invite your team**

1. Log in at [**inbox.messagedesk.com**](http://inbox.messagedesk.com).
2. In the left-hand menu, click **Team**.
3. Select **Invite Teammate** and send invites to everyone who will use Inbox by MessageDesk.

**Why this matters:** We'll only move your number **after** your team is in place. That way, messages are immediately visible to the right people when your number goes live in the Inbox.

### **3. Export contacts from your Reach workspace**

If you'd like to bring existing contacts with you:

1. Sign in to your old account at [**app.messagedesk.com**](http://app.messagedesk.com).
2. Export your contacts (.CSV is great).

![V2 Migration 1 Gi](/images/V2-migration-1.gif)

<Note>
  **Tip:** Take a moment to tidy your list. Remove duplicates and outdated entries to keep Inbox by MessageDesk clean from day one.
</Note>

### **4. Import contacts into Inbox by MessageDesk**

1. Log in at [**inbox.messagedesk.com**](http://inbox.messagedesk.com).
2. Go to **Contacts** and choose **Import Contacts**.
3. Upload your CSV and map the fields (e.g., name, number, email).

![V2 Migration 2 Gi](/images/V2-migration-2.gif)

<Info>
  **Need help?** If you're unsure how your CSV should be formatted, reply to your migration email and attach the file. Our team can review and advise.
</Info>

### **5. Schedule your phone number migration**

Once your team is set up, fill out this form with a preferred time to move your number.

[Schedule Phone Number Migration](https://messagedesk.typeform.com/to/GGtTW64l)

- **Hours:** Monday–Friday, **9:00 am–5:00 pm** PST
- **How to schedule:** Fill out the form with your preferred **day/time** and include your email and the number to move.
- **What to expect:** We'll confirm the window and coordinate so there's little to no downtime. The switch typically takes place inside the scheduled window.

**Important:** We'll only move your number **after Steps 1–4 are complete.**

### **6. (Optional) Download the mobile companion app for iOS or Android**

Stay connected on the go by installing the MessageDesk app on your preferred devices.

https://www.messagedesk.com/mobile 

## **What happens after the switch**

- **Messages to your number** will begin routing into **Inbox by MessageDesk**.
- Your team can now start new conversations, assign conversations, and use templates.
- We'll be on standby during the migration window to make sure everything looks good.

## **FAQs**

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Do I lose my MessageDesk Reach account?">
    No, your MessageDesk Reach account isn't deleted, and data can be moved to Inbox by MessageDesk multiple times. Your original account stays accessible and functional.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I test Inbox by MessageDesk before moving my number over?">
    Yes. If you want to use Inbox by MessageDesk before moving your number, you can, and it's highly recommended. Moving your number to Inbox by MessageDesk is instant, but moving the number back to Reach requires messaging support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What data is migrated during the migration process?">
    The migration transfers your contacts (including addresses and email information), messages (including media attachments), message templates, custom fields, groups (which become labels), and opt-out lists. Every record keeps its original structure and metadata.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="How long does the migration process take?">
    It depends on how much data you have. Data migration runs in the background, and you'll get a notification when it's done. Phone number migration is instant once initiated.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Can I use both my MessageDesk Reach and Inbox by MessageDesk accounts at the same time?">
    Yes. Use both platforms at the same time until you migrate your phone number. Once your phone number is migrated, every text message goes through Inbox by MessageDesk, but your Reach account stays accessible for viewing historical data.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What happens if I need to move my phone number back to MessageDesk Reach?">
    You can always move your phone number back from Inbox by MessageDesk if you choose. Moving the number back requires contacting support.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will there be any downtime during migration?">
    No. There's no downtime during the data migration process. Your MessageDesk Reach account stays fully functional. Phone number migration happens instantly.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="What if something goes wrong during migration?">
    Contact support immediately. Your data stays safe in your MessageDesk Reach account, and you can retry the migration. Data migration can run multiple times if needed.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Do I need to do anything after the migration is complete?">
    Once data migration completes, verify that your contacts, messages, and templates transferred correctly in Inbox by MessageDesk. When you're satisfied, migrate your phone number whenever you're ready.
  </Accordion>
  <Accordion title="Will my team members and permissions be migrated?">
    No. Team members and user permissions are managed separately in Inbox by MessageDesk. Invite your team to your new Inbox by MessageDesk workspace and configure their permissions there.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
