# How to automate with Relays

Relays are MessageDesk's workflow engine. They listen for **events**, evaluate **conditions**, and run **actions** so the right conversation reaches the right person at the right time.

<Warning>
  **Desktop only:** Relays are currently only available on desktop. Mobile users can't access or manage Relays at this time.
</Warning>

<Note>
  **Permissions required:** By default, only **Admin** users have access to Relays. Admins can grant **Relay Management** permissions to other user roles via **Settings → Team Management**. Learn more: [Team Management](/settings/workspace-settings/team-management).
</Note>

## Why use Relays?

- **Scale without chaos:** Auto-assign and auto-label to keep your inbox tidy as volume grows.
- **Respond faster:** Send **after-hours** and **first-touch** replies automatically.
- **Reduce busywork:** Route by **phone line**, **label**, or **teammate** so work starts in the right place.
- **Stay consistent:** Standardize rules so there are no missed handoffs.

---

## What you can do with Relays

1. **Out-of-office auto-reply:** Send an automatic response outside your business hours.
2. **Keyword replies:** Trigger a preset message when a contact texts a specific keyword.
3. **Identify unknown contacts:** Detect anonymous conversations and request name/phone/email automatically.
4. **Auto-assign:** Assign new conversations to one or more teammates as they arrive.
5. **Auto-label:** Apply labels (e.g., _Support_, _HR: New Hire_, _Region: West_) based on line, content, or rules.
6. **Custom Relays:** Combine your own conditions and actions to match your workflow.

<Tip>
  All Relay templates are customizable. You can adjust wording, labels, assignees, active hours, and more.
</Tip>

## How Relays work (at a glance)

![Relay Example Pn](/images/relay-example.png)

**Relays = Triggers → Conditions → Actions**

- **Triggers:** When it runs.\
  There are seven triggers: **Message Received**, **Message Sent**, **Conversation Opened**, **Conversation Users Assigned**, **Conversation Label Applied**, **Comment Created**, and **Conversation Closed**. See [Relay building blocks](/relays/building-blocks#triggers-7) for the full reference.
- **Conditions:** Where/when it applies.\
  _Examples:_\
  • **My phone number equals** _Support_\
  • **Is anonymous conversation** is **true**\
  • **Is unassigned conversation** is **true**\
  • **Active hours** with **Trigger outside of business hours** enabled
- **Actions:** What happens.\
  _Examples:_\
  • **Assign teammates to the conversation**\
  • **Add labels to the conversation**\
  • **Reply to message** (with templates, tags, media, and an optional Delay)\
  • **Open the conversation** or **Close the conversation**

You can **pause** and **resume** any Relay without deleting it.

<Note>
  **Multiple Relays on one event.** When several Relays share the same trigger, **all matching Relays run** in the **Order** you set on the Relays list, top to bottom. Reorder Relays to control sequencing when conditions overlap. For example, run a specific keyword auto-reply before a generic after-hours reply. See [Order your Relays](/relays/create-edit#1a-order-your-relays).
</Note>

<Tip>
  For the full list of triggers, conditions, and actions (with the operator selector on **Conversation label equals** and the phone-line scoping model), see [Relay building blocks](/relays/building-blocks).
</Tip>

## Starter Relay templates

A few common patterns to get you started:

- **After-hours responder**\
  _Trigger:_ Message Received → _Conditions:_ **Active hours** with **Trigger outside of business hours** enabled →\
  _Actions:_ **Reply to message** with "We'll reply at 8am PT. Reply STOP to opt out."
- **New lead triage**\
  _Trigger:_ Message Received → _Conditions:_ **Is anonymous conversation** is true →\
  _Actions:_ **Reply to message** with an info-capture prompt (include "Reply STOP to opt out.") → **Add labels to the conversation** (_Lead_) → **Assign teammates to the conversation** (_Sales Queue_)
- **Phone-line-based routing**\
  _Trigger:_ Message Received → _Conditions:_ **My phone number equals** _Dispatch Line_ →\
  _Actions:_ **Assign teammates to the conversation** (_Dispatcher_) → **Add labels to the conversation** (_Route Change_)

<Tip>
  See the full [Relay template library](/relays/templates) for ready-to-use configurations covering customer service, operations and routing, and webhook integrations. For a reference of every trigger, condition, and action, see [Relay building blocks](/relays/building-blocks).
</Tip>

<Note>
  **Auto-replies don't mark conversations as Read.** When a Relay sends a message, the conversation moves to **Replied** status (so it leaves the Unread filter), but it stays unreviewed until a teammate opens it in the Inbox. See [Relays and conversation status](/relays/create-edit#relays-and-conversation-status).
</Note>
