**Basic Conversation Filters**

- **Newest**: The most recent conversations in your inbox
- **Oldest:** The oldest conversations in your inbox
- **Waiting Longest**: Conversations with the greatest inactivity period
- **Unread First**: A view of all unread conversations

## How to Apply a Custom Filter to an Inbox View

You can filter a conversation based on its applied labels, assignee, or the phone line on which the conversation took place.
