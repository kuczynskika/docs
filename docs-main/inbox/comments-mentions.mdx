## What Are Comments?

- **Internal-only notes** saved inside a text thread

## Comment Threads

Use **comment threads** to reply directly to a specific comment or message, keeping related discussions organized in one place.

### What are comment threads?

- **Threaded replies** let you respond to a specific comment or message
- Keeps conversations **organized** by grouping related comments together
- Available on both **desktop** (hover menu) and **mobile** (force touch bottom sheet)

![Comment Thread Pn](/docs-main/images/comment-thread.png)

### How to reply in a thread

1. **Hover over a comment or message** (desktop) or **force touch** (mobile) to reveal the action menu.
2. Select the **Reply** option to start or continue a thread.
3. Type your reply and click **Send**.

<Note>
  The reply option appears on the **parent comment/message** and the **last comment** in an existing thread. Both options add your reply to the same thread.
</Note>

![New Comment Thread Posted Pn](/docs-main/images/new-comment-thread-posted.png)

### When to use comment threads

- **Follow-up questions:** Reply directly to a teammate's comment for clarification.
- **Status updates:** Thread replies to keep progress notes grouped together.
- **Focused discussions:** Keep side conversations from cluttering the main thread.

## Tips & Best Practices

- **Keep one comment = one ask.** Makes it easier to scan and act.
- **Summarize before you @.** Add 1‚Äì2 lines of context and the next step.
- **Mention the owner.** Use @ on the assignee so nothing slips through.
- **Attach artifacts.** Drop photos, PDFs, or screenshots right in the comment.
- **Close the loop.** Post a short ‚ÄúResolved‚Äîclosing thread‚Äù comment before you close.

## Privacy & Visibility

- Comments are **never** sent to contacts.
- Mentions **notify teammates** (via email) and link back to the exact thread.
- Comments live **inside the conversation** for full history and auditing.

## Emoji Reactions

Add emoji reactions to messages and non-threaded comments to quickly acknowledge, respond, or express sentiment without typing a full reply.

### What are emoji reactions?

- **Quick responses** to messages and comments using emojis
- Available on **messages** and **non-threaded comments**
- Displayed in the **top-left** of the message or comment with a count of unique reactions

### How to add an emoji reaction

1. **Hover over a message or comment** (desktop) to reveal the action menu.
2. Select the **emoji reaction** option.
3. Choose an emoji to add your reaction.

### Viewing reactions

- Reactions appear in the **top-left corner** of the message or comment
- A **number** displays the count of unique emoji reactions
- **Click the reaction** to open a list showing all emoji reactions and the users who posted them

## FAQ

**Do comments send as texts to the customer?**\
No. Comments are internal-only.

**How do I know I‚Äôm commenting, not texting?**\
The message compose box turns **yellow,** and the **Message type** shows **Comment**.

**Can I mention multiple teammates at once?**\
Yes. Select as many as you need.

**Can I attach media to a comment?**\
Yes. Use the **\+** button in the comment composer.

**Will I get notified if someone mentions me?**\
Yes. Mentions trigger an **email notification** with a link to the thread.

**How do I reply to a specific comment or message?**\
Hover over the comment or message (desktop) and select **Reply** to add your response to that thread.

**Can I find all comments where I‚Äôm mentioned?**\
Yes. In **My Inbox**, use the pre-saved view for **comments you‚Äôre mentioned in**.
