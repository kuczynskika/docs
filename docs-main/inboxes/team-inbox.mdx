## Inviting Teammates

You can invite teammates by clicking `Invite Teammate` from the inbox side navigation. Or you can follow the steps here: [How to invite teammates](https://messagedesk.mintlify.app/get-started/teammates).

## Assigning Teammates to Conversations

You can assign conversations to teammates by clicking the `person` icon in the top-right corner of the conversation.

- You can assign 1 or many teammates to a conversation.
- A teammate will always have access to conversations they are assigned to.

![Clean Shot2025 08 26at09 51 01 Pn](/images/CleanShot2025-08-26at09.51.01.png)

## Teammate Inboxes

Assigned conversations automatically get routed to the teammate's inbox. Individually, we can view our own conversations by clicking `My Conversations` under `My Inbox.`

To view other teammate conversations, simply select them from the Inbox menu on the left.

![Team Inbox Outline Pn](/images/team-inbox-outline.png)

## Grant and Revoke Acess

You can create custom user roles to grant or revoke acces to specific teammate inboxes
