## Overview

MessageDesk offers flexible pricing plans designed to scale with your business needs. Choose the plan that best fits your team size and messaging volume.

## Plans

### Free trial

Start with a free trial to explore MessageDesk features:
- 14-day trial period
- Full access to core features
- No credit card required
- Easy upgrade to paid plan

### Pricing tiers

Contact our sales team to discuss pricing options that fit your business needs.

## Billing

### Payment methods

We accept major credit cards and ACH payments for annual plans.

### Billing cycle

- Monthly billing: Charged on the same day each month
- Annual billing: Charged once per year with discounted rates

## Frequently asked questions

### Can I change my plan?

Yes, you can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time from your account settings.

### What happens when my trial ends?

When your trial ends, you'll need to select a paid plan to continue using MessageDesk. Your data will be preserved for 30 days.

### Do you offer refunds?

We offer refunds on a case-by-case basis. Contact our support team to discuss your situation.

Have questions about pricing or billing? [Contact our sales team](https://www.messagedesk.com/contact) or reach out to [support](/contact-us).
