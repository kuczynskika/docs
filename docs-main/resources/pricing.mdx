### Pricing

Per-user, per-month pricing. Save when you choose yearly billing.

| Plan       | Monthly billing (per user / month) | Yearly billing (per user / month) | Best for                                                  |
| ---------- | ---------------------------------- | --------------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------- |
| Team       | \$39                               | \$29                              | Teams that need the power of a shared team SMS inbox      |
| Pro        | \$99                               | \$79                              | Teams that need to automate, integrate, & scale messaging |
| Enterprise | [Let's Chat](/contact-us)          | [Let's Chat](/contact-us)         | Larger organizations with more advanced needs             |

- **Monthly plans:** Annual contract billed monthly (Team and Pro).
- **Yearly plans:** Annual contract billed annually for extra savings (Team, Pro, and Enterprise).

All prices in USD. Applicable taxes are added at checkout.

#### Team plan highlights

- Per user seat pricing
- Connect up to 48 phone numbers
- Unlimited 1-on-1 SMS texting
- Carrier registration included
- Label, assign, comment & mention

#### Pro plan highlights

Everything in Team, plus:

- Unlimited 100-person text broadcasts
- Custom roles & permissions
- Multi-line opt-out management
- Relays (automations)
- Zapier integration

#### Enterprise plan highlights

Everything in Pro, plus:

- Priority support
- Multiple inboxes & workspaces
- Custom SSO
- Custom integrations
- Data & analytics dashboards

## Why our pricing model works

Text messaging for teams shouldn't feel like solving a math problem. Other SMS inboxes hide fees, charge per message, charge per phone line, or sell bundles of "credits" you don't need.

MessageDesk keeps it simple: one flat, per-user rate.

| MessageDesk                                | Other SMS inboxes                          |
| ------------------------------------------ | ------------------------------------------ |
| Unlimited 1-on-1 SMS texting               | "Credit-based" texting                     |
| No overage charges                         | Additional overage charges                 |
| No fees for added phone lines              | Pricing per phone line                     |
| Keep your voice provider                   | Provider lock-in                           |
| No setup fees                              | Additional setup fees                      |
| No hidden charges                          | Hidden charges                             |
| Cancel anytime                             | Cancellation fees                          |
| No minimum commitments                     | Minimum commitments                        |
| Carrier registration included              | Additional charges for carrier registration |

## Additional costs

There are currently no additional costs beyond your user seat price.

Every MessageDesk subscription includes carrier registration and phone number configuration fees baked into your per-seat price.

### Tax calculations

- **Based on billing address**: Taxes calculated from payment method address.
- **Variable rates**: Different services may have different tax rates.
- **Compliance updates**: Tax policies may change to meet legal requirements.
