# Quick Search

Quick Search is the fastest way to find a **contact** or **conversation** in MessageDesk. It lives in the top nav bar on desktop and is built for keyboard-first navigation.

<Note>
  Quick Search is **desktop-only**. On mobile and tablet, tap the search icon near the account settings icon to open [Advanced Search](/search/advanced-search) directly.
</Note>

---

## Open Quick Search

There are two ways to open Quick Search on desktop:

- Press **⌘K** (macOS) or **Ctrl K** (Windows/Linux) from anywhere in the app.
- Click the **search bar** in the top nav.

---

## What Quick Search covers

Quick Search returns results for two item types:

- **Contacts** — saved contact records in your workspace
- **Conversations** — text threads in your inbox

<Note>
  **Messages** and **comments** are **not** in Quick Search results. To search by message content, comment content, or labels, use [Advanced Search](/search/advanced-search).
</Note>

---

## Recent conversations

Before you type anything, Quick Search shows your **recent conversations** so you can jump back to a thread you were just in—no query required.

---

## Navigate results with the keyboard

- **↑ / ↓** — move between results
- **Enter** — select the highlighted result

Selecting a result with **Enter** opens [Advanced Search](/search/advanced-search) **at that result**, giving you the full filtered experience as a next step.

---

## Open Advanced Search from Quick Search

At the bottom of the Quick Search results dropdown, click **Open Advanced Search** to escalate your current query into the full Advanced Search experience—useful when you want to search messages, comments, or labels, or apply per-type filters.

You can also click the **search icon** next to any Quick Search result to jump straight into Advanced Search at that result.
